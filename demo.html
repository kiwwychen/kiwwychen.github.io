Introduction
We have developed a website that highlights the importance
of the ocean, using simple and easily understandable
language for explanations. The website is designed with three
main subsections, each focusing on the significance
of phytoplankton, animals, and the aquatic environment to the
ecosystem. In the section about marine life, we
metaphorically depict the ocean as a universe where different
deities govern various aspects based on their characteristics
and behaviors.

The importance of the ocean that we introduce includes its
role in carbon cycling, maintaining temperature stability,
and providing essential marine services that even people living
inland benefit from. We utilize videos and the website's
format to effectively convey our content, and we enhance
engagement through interactive games.

While not everyone may directly derive benefits from the
ocean, it has a profound impact on the environment in which
humans live. The website we've designed uses straightforward
data and explanations to help people who may not have had
previous exposure to the ocean gain a better understanding
of it. It serves an educational purpose, aiming
to raise awareness about the ocean's significance.
